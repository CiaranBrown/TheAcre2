About Us

Meet The Team

The idea of The Acre started during late in the Coronavirus pandemic lockdown when Sean Brown and his nephew Thomas Kelly noticed that the previous owners was stepping down. Sean being a successful entrepreneur with being the owners of thriving local businesses, took advantage of the situation and met up with Thomas and talked with current manager Laura Kelly (niece of Sean and sister of Thomas) whose both had a dream of running a pub, thought hard about the idea.

After weeks of consideration and planning, they started to redesign and refurbish the inside and outisde of the pub. Managing to get it finished by the time lockdown measures were restricted for pubs to reopen. Within the first few weeks, the pub was extremely busy from start to finish. Over a year later and The Acre is now granted as one of the best pubs to eat and drink in March.
