Food

View Our Menu's

We partenered up with local small business Barney's Bistro which originally catered a burger van outside different locations. The Acre & Barney's Bistro made partnership in Summer 2021 and we started serving food at the start of August and quicky became very excited to bring on a new chapter. Over a year has gone by and we are now rated the #2 best place to eat in March on Tripadvisor. We are very proud and honoured to have Barney and his team provide their incredible food for our pub.

Sunday Roast

In the first few weeks of serving food, after receiving so many phone calls about the suggestion of serving Sunday Lunch. With such a high demand, Barney started planning and organising. Since Barney's first week serving Sunday lunch, he has been fully booked nearly every week receiving clean plates and positive feedback. If you are wanting to book a table for Sunday, booking is highly advisable to avoid dissapointment.
