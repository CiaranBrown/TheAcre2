Welcome to The Acre

We are a small, family run pub located in the centre of March, Cambridgeshire. We pride ourselves in giving our customers a friendly welcome, safe environment and to always have a good visit.

Our Drinks On Draught

We provide you with a range of the best lagers, cider and ales on draught from our brewery Greene King.

Madri

Our newest line on draught! Crisp, clean and refreshing. Come and try a pint of this full of flavour and aroma Spanish style lager.

Guinness

Being the most popular drink on draught across the world, this traditional Irish Stout is truly unique & perfectly balanced. "There's nothing on this planet like a pint of the black stuff."

Aspall

Our only draught cider but easily the best option. Brewed in Suffolk, Aspall Cyder is a crisp, medium dry cider with a delicate aroma and taste of fresh pressed apples.
